Covid Rates

The COVID-19 pandemic had shaken the entire world, with each country being hit to a different extent. Thanks to the data collected by Our World in Data, we have all the information necessary to calculate which countries were most affected, the percentage of the population affected per country, and more. All calculations are done through T-SQL and are uploaded through Github. You can find the links to each section of queries in the menu on the top right corner.

Now, with the data being prepped and organized, we can upload them to Excel sheets and send them over to Tableau.
